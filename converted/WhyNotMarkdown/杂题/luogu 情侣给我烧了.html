设F(S)为x\in S\iff (a_x,b_x)\in R\lor (b_x,a_x)\in R的方案R计数(其中S是1..n的子集). 设G(S)为x\in S\implies (a_x,b_x)\in R\lor (b_x,a_x)\in R的方案R计数. 有G(S)=\sum_{S\subseteq T}F(T)反演有F(S)=\sum_{S\subseteq T}(-1)^{|T-S|}G(T).

对于一个S设k=|S|有G(S)=\binom{n}{k}k!2^k\times (2n-2k)!有 \begin{aligned} F(S) &=\sum_{i=k}^n(-1)^{i-k}\binom{n-k}{i-k}\ \left(\binom{n}{i}i!2^i\times (2n-2i)!\right)\\ &=n!(n-k)!\sum_{i=k}^n\frac{(-1)^{i-k}}{(i-k)!}\times \frac{2^i(2n-2i)!}{((n-i)!)^2}\\ \text{let}&\ a_i=\frac{(-1)^i}{i!},\ b_{n-i}=2^i\frac{(2n-2i)!}{((n-i)!)^2},\ b_i=2^{n-i}\frac{(2i)!}{(i!)^2}\\ F(S)&=n!(n-k)!\sum_{i=k}^na_{i-k}b_{n-i}=n!(n-k)!\sum_{i=0}^{n-k}a_{n-k-i}b_{i} \end{aligned}

最后对于k的答案就是|S|=k的S计算\binom{n}{k}F(S).
